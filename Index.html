About Us

Discover our story in providing Events Services.

Our Story

“Not all angels have wings, some have stethoscopes”. This is our initiative to acknowledge and recognize 150 of the Best Individuals/Brands across Europe, Middle East and Asia that are the forgotten soldiers of the Medical Community.

Our Mission

This is one of a kind award show where in we aim to give you once in a life time experience. Our endeavor is to recognize the best in the medical fraternity. Their contribution towards the wellbeing of our society is often overlooked and goes unrecognized.

Our Vision

This is our humble way to give back to the community which goes through sleepless nights and works tirelessly, round the clock for our better health. Happening for the first time ever in UAE but certainly not the last.
